ABOUT US
 

AKB Institute of Finance & Management is a Pioneer Institution for Financial and Taxation courses. It is offering various courses in the field of Finance , Taxation , import-export, Law and Management courses. It's objective is to provide knowledge in various field of modern business. The purposes of AKB Institute of Finance & Management is to encourage freedom of inquiry and a free exchange of ideas among learners, faculty, administrators and staff of AKB Institute of Finance & Management.

 
     
 

AKB Institute of Finance & Management (AKBIFM) has, since its inception in 1997, made rapid strides in providing quality education to thousand of students. The student enrollment has grown exponentially .Our global family consists of students from all states of India, representing varied education and cultural backgrounds. One of the main focuses of the Institute at this time is in the area of professional & skill oriented technologies to the masses. We believe that real, life-changing learning can only take place in an environment of academic excellence, challenge, and engagement.

 
     
 

HISTORY: -

 
 

AKBIFM has a history of meeting the need of corporate India. AKBIFM has always been a first one to introduce unplanned management programs with an objective to fulfill corporate needs. AKBIFM has always emphasized the need for a new direction to Accounting, Taxation and Management Education through unique learning methodology combining all three forms of learning, namely, the book, e- learning & personnel contact programme. Merely putting people through a standard educational procedure at an early age is not enough. Instead the educational system needs to be flexible. It should be able to identify varied educational needs of the people at different stages in their career and also provide appropriate and adequate means/opportunity to cater to these needs. This objective is gratifying by AKBIFM.
 
     
 

DISTINGUISING OURSELVES: -

 
     
 

AKBIFM's history is long and distinguished, its momentum is strong, and, with the support of those who know and love it so well, AKBIFM will continue to achieve the highest level of excellence. Over the past 10 years, we have accomplished a great deal, including expanding and enhancing our faculty, growing and improving the quality and diversity of our student body, and upgrading and adding to our living and learning facilities.
