MISSION

  
 

Our mission is to provide quality education to meet the emerging challenges of business environment by providing practical knowledge in various fields. This mission is fulfilled through innovative programs that are responsive to the needs of students and involve engaging and relevant learning experiences offered in a variety of delivery modes. AKBIFM believes that all qualified mid-career professionals, regardless of their geographic location, have an equal right to participate in institutions of higher education. AKBIFM offers this opportunity within an environment that stimulates personal, professional, and leadership growth.

 

 
  VISION

  
 

Our vision is to create a campus where anyone is able to achieve higher education and enlightened career. Our motto is to develop confidence and competence in every student through the means of Distance Education learning so that they will able to handle all aspects in their relevant fields of study. “Our motto is giving the Best.” AKB Institute of Finance & Management provides tomorrow's successful professionals with the knowledge, real-life skills and practical background required for today's demanding job market.
